Help Page

Need Help?

1. Getting Started

Don't know how to start?

Step 1: Sign up or Sign in your account. Just click the “Sign Up” button on the home page or the User button on the top right of the home page.

Step 2: Set up your Gcash Account or PayMaya. Make sure that you put in exact information and number to avoid payment and account errors.

Sign Up

Step 3: In this stage, you can purchase any items that you want or need either for academics, professional, or personal use.

2. Account Setting

To Update Your Profile

  • Click your profile icon on the top right.
  • Select Account Settings.
  • Edit your name, email, or password.
  • Click Save Changes.

To Change Password

  • Go to Account Settings → Security.
  • Click Change Password.
  • Enter your old password and new one.
  • Click Save.

To Log Out

  • Click your profile icon.
  • Select Logout.
  • You'll be signed out of your account.
3. Guide

How to Order

  • Browse items from the Shop page.
  • Click “Add to Cart” on the items you want.
  • Go to your Cart and click “Checkout.”
  • Enter your delivery details and payment method.
  • Click “Place Order” to confirm.

How to Track Orders

  • Go to My Orders under your account.
  • Click the order you want to track.
  • View the delivery status and tracking details.

How to Contact Support

  • Go to the Help section or scroll to the page bottom.
  • Click “Contact Us.”
  • Choose Email or Chat Support to reach our team.
4. Purchasing and Payments

Don’t know how to buy and order?

Step 1: To order online, you have to set up your Gcash account or PayMaya or both of them for convenience.

Step 2: Make sure to fill out the exact information needed when setting up your account.

Step 3: Before purchasing a product, make sure your Gcash and PayMaya has more than enough money to buy the product.
